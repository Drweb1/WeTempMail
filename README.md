# WeTempMail - Disposable Temporary Email Service

Our WeTempMail service provides you with a secure, anonymous, and free disposable email address. Designed to keep your primary inbox spam-free, our platform allows you to receive emails instantly without any registration. Whether you are testing a website or signing up for a service, our temporary mail ensures your real identity remains protected from hackers and advertisers.

WeTempMail is a free temporary email service that actually handles file attachments — unlike most temp mail services that strip PDFs, images, and ZIP files.

🌐 **Website:** [wetempmail.com](https://wetempmail.com)

---

## ✨ Features

- 📎 **Full Attachment Support** — Download PDFs, images, videos, and ZIP files without any issues
- ✏️ **Custom Email Addresses** — Choose your own username instead of random strings
- ⚡ **Instant Delivery** — Emails arrive within seconds
- 🚫 **No Signup Required** — No registration, no tracking, no ads
- 🕒 **Auto-Delete After 24 Hours** — Designed with privacy in mind

---

## 🚀 Why WeTempMail?

Most temporary email services strip attachments completely. You receive the email, but the files are gone.

WeTempMail solves this. It works like a real email service — but with complete privacy.

| Feature                                | Other Temp Mails     | WeTempMail              |
|----------------------------------------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Attachments (PDFs, images, ZIP)        | ❌ Stripped          | ✅ Downloadable         |
| Custom Username                        | ❌ Random only       | ✅ Choose yours         |
| Signup Required                        | ❌ Sometimes         | ✅ Never                |
| Ads & Tracking                         | ❌ Often             | ✅ None                 |
| Auto-Delete                            | ⚠️ Varies            | ✅ 24 hours             |
